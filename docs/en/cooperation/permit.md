# Get a license
## Overview
1. Cloud-PE plugins are free to use for non-profit, non-commercial projects that have no ads, no charges and no bundling — no extra license required. Profit-making commercial projects must apply to the Cloud-PE team for a license. Only licensed projects are officially recognized by Cloud-PE.
2. Cloud-PE charges nothing for licensing, whether for non-profit or commercial projects.
3. If a developer modifies and releases the Cloud-PE kernel, they must clearly state that the kernel is a modified version based on Cloud-PE, and apply for the corresponding license. Works that do not follow the license terms will face infringement claims.
4. Important: Cloud-PE licenses are non-transferable.
5. The Cloud-PE team reserves the right to revoke a permanent license at any time without prior notice to the project owner.

## How licensing works
No paper or electronic certificate is issued. License information is published in the sub-sections of this chapter; each licensed project gets its own page showing its license details.

## How to get a license
Join our QQ group and @ the group owner to request a license. See [here](https://cloud-pe.cn/qqg) for details.

## License scope
Licensing covers, but is not limited to:
- Use of the Cloud-PE plugin loader
- Use of the Cloud-PE plugin market
- Use of the Cloud-PE name for promotion

## License term
Normally, licenses granted by the Cloud-PE team are long-term or permanent. However, if the terms are violated or a license is revoked for other reasons, we will generally notify the project owner via QQ, email, etc. (except in special cases). After revocation, the owner must remove all content that requires a Cloud-PE license within 7 days (from the revocation date), otherwise it will be treated as infringement.
